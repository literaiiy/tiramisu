Welcome to tiramisu!

tiramisu is a Hypixel player statistics site that gives a comprehensive outlook on your Minecraft account and Hypixel profile- complete name history, detailed breakdowns of your Hypixel statistics (including an elaborate analysis of your SkyWars and BedWars stats), among other things. It is currently in open beta, and there are bound to be a lot of bugs, but I am actively working on resolving these bugs and improving the experience tiramisu provides.

FAQ

How is your site different from other stats sites?

  • I try to add unique stats that other sites may not have. Sometimes they're more fun than practical or useful, but if it's there, it's there for a reason. While I see myself in the same realm as other stats sites, they're all great and are unique in their own way.

    • What is a UUID?

  • A UUID, or Universally Unique IDentifier, is a unique string of characters that is tied directly to a Minecraft account. Your UUID is attached to your account from the date of your account's registration and doesn't change, making it more reliable than a username to identify an individual to tie stats to.

    • Will you ever put ads?

  • No. tiramisu is ad-free and plans to never change that.

    • Are you stealing my data?

  • Also- no. This is covered in the Privacy Policy. I don't take any personally identifiable information, or share anything with third parties.

    • Why don't you have (this feature)?

  • tiramisu is a relatively new project. I am actively working on it as a project alongside school and other things, but most major features will be added as soon as possible.

    • Is tiramisu open-source?

  • Not at the moment.

    • Why is there a '>' symbol in front of my first name's duration had?

  • Mojang doesn't provide an account's creation date in the API, so if an account's Hypixel join date is before their first name change, it will be used as a substitute for a pseudo account creation date. This means that number is almost certainly not an accurate figure.

    • What is the "Hypixel ____" on my profile?

  • The title is entirely based on how long you have been playing on the server- or more accurately, the amount of time since your first login.

    • Why don't the player counts instantly update every time I refresh?

  • Player counts only update once every 3 seconds to avoid spam and running over the Hypixel API's rate limit of 120 requests per minute.

    • Why can't I search for a name I've previously had?

  • At some point in November of 2020, Mojang stopped support for querying a UUID to receive a username at a certain timestamp, making it impossible to find a player's current name from a previous one. Sorry about that. Source: Mojang API Documentation. See this ticket for more information on the topic.
